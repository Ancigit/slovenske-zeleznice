Traction

Services in traction include crew assignment to train operating companies for various rail freight and passenger operations such as haulage and shunting operations at fixed shunting locations.

We also perform tasks – for our partner Slovenske železnice –  which support the core activities above:

  • Maintenance-related technical tasks,
  • Scheduling of locomotives and locomotive staff,
  • Training,
  • Production of traction rules and assistance in producing national safety regulations,
  • Tasks relating to traction costs and holding energy efficiency courses for operations staff,
  • Tasks relating to supervision of locomotive staff and other miscellaneous tasks required in connection with other rail activities.
